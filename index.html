Instructions


Guess the correct word! You have 5 health points - you lose your points if you type in an incorrect letter. If you lose all the points, the game is over. But don't worry! Every word comes with a hint displayed at the top of the page, and even so, if the answer still too difficult to guess, you can always draw another one by pressing the "change" button!

Important: The answers contain only letters - if you type any other character, you lose your health point as well!

GOOD LUCK!
